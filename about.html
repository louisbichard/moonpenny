I have been a qualified therapist for well over 20 years and I am registered with the Federation of Holistic Therapists and I am also a registered mental health nurse with the Nursing and Midwifery Council.

I provide premium bespoke Hypnotherapy with Aromatherapy and NLP utilising the psychosensory therapy of Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT.)

I am really looking forward to helping you transform your life for the better. Just imagine being able to let go of fear, anxieties, traumas and limiting beliefs and gaining personal freedom to live your life better.

Why see me?

I was introduced to the idea of Holistic Therapy in 1988 through being employed in the health and beauty sector where I met and attended exclusive training sessions with Bharti Vyas 30 years ago in her London clinic. Bharti was one of the first people to embrace holistic therapy and is the vice president of the HBEF – Health and Beauty Employers Federation.

It was here that I first recognised the importance of holistic therapy which is the philosophy of aiming to treat a client as a whole rather than being separated into different parts which do not interconnect. The individual’s body, spirit and mind is treated. I have personally overcome many fears, anxieties and trauma and if I can learn how to do this so can you. All of these were in my mind and were so real, I allowed these fears and negative thoughts to gain control until I learnt how to change my state of mind and gain personal freedom. Our minds are very powerful and as a result we can spend much of our lives in an unnecessary state such as states of anxiety, depression, stress and panic feeling stuck and helpless and unable to leave the past.

Many of us go through life with self-limiting negative beliefs instead of choosing to enjoy living in the moment, to be able to be in a better state, be excited, motivated, and happy with hope and joy and be at peace.

We can change and remove the unnecessary programmes that we run in our heads, often with help very quickly.

My mission is to help and guide as many people as I can to be in the right mind-set, to overcome stress, anxiety, fears and phobias and how to enhance your own life so you get the most out of it and more!
